About me

My name is Cuong Ly. I am currently a PhD student at University of Utah working with Dr. Tolga Tasdizen

Research

My research interests include Image Analysis, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. Specifically, I am currently interested in synthesizing realistic images via texture descriptions. The progress in this area will help to us to gain a better understanding of texture perception and close the gap between computer vision and human perception.
